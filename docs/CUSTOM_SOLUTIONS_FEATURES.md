## Overview

Finlo Custom Solutions delivers a fully white-labeled, AI-powered financial intelligence platform tailored to your business needs. Each solution includes personalized branding, dedicated feature modules, and flexible credit allocations.

---

## Core Platform (Included in All Solutions)

### 🤖 AI CFO Chat
Your always-available financial advisor powered by advanced AI.
- **Natural Language Queries**: Ask questions in plain English about your business finances
- **Real-Time Analysis**: Instant insights from your connected financial data
- **Document Intelligence**: Upload invoices, reports, and statements for AI-powered analysis
- **Voice Input**: Speak your questions with built-in transcription
- **Context-Aware Responses**: AI understands your industry, company size, and business model

### 📊 Financial Dashboard
Comprehensive visibility into your business health.
- **Cash Flow Forecasting**: Predictive runway analysis with monthly projections
- **AR Intelligence**: Accounts receivable aging, collection risk, and payment predictions
- **Expense Analysis**: Top vendor spending, category breakdowns, and trend detection
- **Customer Profitability**: Revenue concentration, lifetime value, and growth metrics
- **Interactive Charts**: Cash flow trends, customer revenue, and expense distribution

### 🔗 Accounting Integrations
Seamless connection to your existing tools.
- **QuickBooks Online**: Full sync of customers, invoices, expenses, and accounts
- **Wave Accounting**: Complete financial data import and analysis
- **Zoho Books**: Enterprise accounting integration
- **Automatic Sync**: Keep data fresh with scheduled refreshes

### 👥 Team Collaboration
Work together with your team.
- **Unlimited Team Members**: Invite colleagues with role-based access
- **Shared Conversations**: Team-wide visibility into AI chat history
- **Document Sharing**: Collaborative access to uploaded financial documents
- **Activity Tracking**: See what your team is analyzing

### 🎨 White-Label Branding
Your brand, your platform.
- **Custom Logo**: Replace Finlo branding with your company logo
- **Branded Experience**: Users see your identity throughout the application
- **Professional URLs**: Custom domain support available

## Add-On Feature Modules

### 💰 Competitive Pricing Module
*Multi-supplier price comparison and market intelligence*

**Capabilities:**
- Upload supplier quotes and invoices for automatic extraction
- Compare pricing across unlimited suppliers per product/service
- Track price variance and identify cost-saving opportunities
- AI-powered analysis of pricing trends and recommendations
- Historical price tracking and vendor performance metrics

**Best For:** Procurement teams, purchasing managers, businesses with multiple suppliers

---

### 👤 Customer CRM Module
*Customer relationship management and sales intelligence*

**Capabilities:**
- Centralized customer database with full contact history
- Sales pipeline tracking and forecasting
- Customer communication logs and notes
- Integration with financial data for revenue tracking
- AI-powered customer health scoring

**Best For:** Sales teams, account managers, service businesses

---

### 📦 Inventory Module
*Stock management and supply chain visibility*

**Capabilities:**
- Real-time inventory levels and tracking
- Low stock alerts and reorder recommendations
- Supplier management and lead time tracking
- Cost of goods sold (COGS) analysis
- Inventory valuation and turnover metrics

**Best For:** Retailers, wholesalers, manufacturers, e-commerce

---

### 🧾 Invoicing Module
*Professional invoice generation and payment tracking*

**Capabilities:**
- Customizable invoice templates
- Automated invoice generation and delivery
- Payment status tracking and reminders
- Integration with accounting systems
- Revenue recognition and cash flow forecasting

**Best For:** Service businesses, freelancers, agencies, consultants

---

### 📈 Advanced Reporting Module
*Enhanced analytics and custom report generation*

**Capabilities:**
- Custom report builder with drag-and-drop interface
- Scheduled report delivery via email
- Multi-period comparison and trend analysis
- Export to PDF, Excel, and CSV formats
- KPI dashboards with real-time updates

**Best For:** CFOs, controllers, financial analysts, executives

---

### 💵 Payroll Module *(Coming Soon)*
*Employee compensation and tax management*
